most famouse egyptian food

egyptian food is very tasty and delicious.here are some of the most famous dishes

koshari

koshari is a very popular dish in egypt.it is made from rice,lentils and pasta and is topped with a spicy tomato sauce and fried onions.

ful medames

ful medames is a traditional egyptian breakfast dish.it is made from fava beans that are cooked until soft and then mashed and seasoned with garlic,lemon juice and olive oil.
